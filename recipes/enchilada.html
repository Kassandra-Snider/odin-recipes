Enchilada Bake

This delicious casserole-style enchilada is perfect for a family meal. The prep time for this dish is only 15 minutes, and cook time is 45 minutes.

Ingriedients

  • 1 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 15oz can vegetarian refried beans
  • 1/2 16oz can diced tomatoes and green chiles
  • 8oz sliced seitan
  • 1/2 8oz package shredded mozzerella-style vegan cheese
  • 9 tortillas (6 inch)
  • 1 15oz can green enchilada sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. pour crushed tomatoes into the bottom of a casserole dish. layer 1/3 of the rice, 1/3 of the beans, 1/3 of the diced tomatoes, 1/3 of the seitan, 1/3 of the cheese, 1/3 of the tortillas, and 1/3 of the enchilada sauce into the dish
  3. Repeat step 2 until there are 3 layers total
  4. Bake in the oven until the cheese melts and the casserole is heated through, about 45 minutes.
