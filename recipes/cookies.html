Sugar Cookies

These moist sugar cookies are a wonderful treat! Better yet, they take under 25 minutes to make.

Ingriedients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 (6oz) container soy yogurt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F, lightly grease a baking sheet with cooking spray.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and baking soda into a bowl.
  3. Mix vegetable oil, sugar, and soy yogurt together in a bowl. Stir in flour mixture until dough comes together.
  4. Drop tablespoons of dough 2 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheet
  5. Bake in the oven until golden, about 7 minutes.
